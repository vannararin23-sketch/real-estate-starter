About Us

We exist to serve the property seeker

At Apple Kh, we are committed to redefining the real estate experience in Cambodia by delivering trusted guidance, quality properties, and exceptional service. Whether you're buying, selling, or investing, our team combines local market expertise with a customer-first approach to help you make confident and informed decisions. We focus on long-term value, transparent solutions, and building lasting relationships with our clients. At Apple Kh, your goals are our priority—we turn aspirations into addresses.

Our Vision

At Apple Kh, our vision is to make real estate more accessible and transparent for everyone, especially low- and medium-income individuals and families in Cambodia. Inspired by our founder, an investor from the United States, we aim to solve the common challenges faced by students, workers, and everyday families who struggle to find clear, accurate, and well-organized information when renting or buying a property.
